Durban South Africa Temple

Temple Locale

The Durban South Africa Temple is located north of the Umhlanga city center in the Izinga development. The temple is situated atop a beautiful grassy knoll near the ocean and a highway with unobstructed views to the sugar cane fields for which the area is known.

Temple Facts

The Durban South Africa Temple was the fifth temple built in Africa and the second built in South Africa, following the Johannesburg South Africa Temple (1985).
